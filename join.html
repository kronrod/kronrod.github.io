Membership FAQ

How do I become a member?
Through applying for membership by submitting the membership form below and paying the membership fee. Anyone -- legal entities as well as natural persons -- is allowed to join. However, only members who reside in Switzerland are eligible to vote. If you'd like to join as a corporate member please contact us.

How do I stay a member?
You can renew your membership at any time by renewing your payment and resubmitting this form.

What's the membership fee for individuals?
You are free to choose the amount you are sending us, but it must be at least one Satoshi. We recommend a membership fee of 50 CHF in Bitcons. This will last for one year. As a member you will be listed on our website with a name linked your website (or Twitter account). The listing is voluntary.

What's the membership fee for corporates?
The membership fee is 380 CHF in Bitcoin per calendar year.
